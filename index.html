The Theoretical and Experimental Algorithmics Laboratory (TEALab) is a research lab in the Department of Computer Science at Stony Brook University, USA. The goal of this research team is to design algorithms and develop implementations that support high-performance computing for solving important real-world problems. They work on designing and implementing efficient algorithms for multicores, GPUs, and distributed-memory machines and if possible automates this process. The solutions developed by the team are applicable in fields ranging across computational biology, engineering, and graph theory.
